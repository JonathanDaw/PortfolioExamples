About Me

Hi, I'm Jonathan Daw, the author of The Bison Herd, and I'm here to tell you a little bit about myself. I am twenty-four years old, live in Cary, North Carolina, and plan on becoming a software developer. In 1996, I was born in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, which is where my family lived until I was two years old. In 1998, we moved to Chesapeake, Virginia, where I spent most of my time playing sports like baseball and basketball. In 2005, my father accepted a new position at his work that led us to Clayton, North Carolina. Upon completing high school in 2015, we moved shortly after to Cary, North Carolina, so that my father and I could be closer to our respective jobs. I currently live at home with my mother and father, along with our sweet American Staffordshire Terrier, Ryder. My current focus is on completing my Software Development AAS degree to aide my search for a quality software development position in the Triangle.
