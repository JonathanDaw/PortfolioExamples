The Bison Herd

The Bison Herd features the five unexpected heros of our world, tasked with destroying the evil within Wayne Community College. Unknown to most that pass through the hallways, there are surprises lying in wait, hoping for an opportunity to overtake the school authority and rule the entire college. These individuals band their strengths together to form a Herd of justice in order to protect their fellow students and faculty.
