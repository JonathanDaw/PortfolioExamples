About The Bison Herd

In 2015, students at Wayne Community College began noticing eery events around the hallways. Lockers would open and close on their own, pencils would roll across the hallways, and windows would be found open all over the school, even after security had ensured everything was locked down the previous day. To most of the students, this seemed odd, but not too suspicious. To five young men, however, they could each sense a storm brewing within the hallways that was unlike anything the school had seen before. These individuals could sense paranormal spirits that had been unleashed and were threatening the peace of all those that attend Wayne Community College. Using their powers combined, these men formed The Bison Herd with their goal of cleansing the school of this newfound evil.
The Bison Herd is composed of these five brave, extraordinary people:

  • Mr. Gargantuan, the Man with UNKNOWN Levels of Strength
  • Flame, Master of Fire
  • MysterioX, the Calm, Mysterious, Mind Bending Hero
  • Bossman, the Methodical Man in Charge
  • Blue Ranger, the Military-Trained Combat Hero
The Herd combines their various strengths together to investigate the source of the seemingly paranormal activity. Bossman acts as the Boss and strings an organized plan together to direct the group in a direction to figure out what in the world is going on. MysterioX and the Blue Ranger are grouped together because the Ranger can protect MysterioX while he is using his gifted mind powers to investigate the college. MysterioX finds the otherworldly beings that are maneuvering throughout the school, and begins tracking their movements. This tracking leads the Herd to a forgotten, sealed door beneath one of the buildings. Thanks to Mr. Gargantuan, the sealed door proves to be nothing more than a slight obstacle and is quickly ripped off the hinges. Behind the door, the leader of the ghastly beings awaits. What ensues is a battle equivalent to the final fight in the Avengers: End Game. Unfortunately for the ghasts, fire is their key weakness, which allows Flame to quickly dispose of these beings. Due to the quick response of The Bison Herd, other students never knew of a potential threat to their well-being. Moving forward, MysterioX will patrol the college on a weekly basis to ensure no other beings attempt to compromise their colleagues' safety.
